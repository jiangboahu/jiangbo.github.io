Biography

I received the B.S. degrees in mathematics and applied mathematics and the M.Eng. and Ph.D. degree in computer science from[Anhui University] of China in 2009, 2012, and 2015, respectively. I am currently a professor in [School of Computer Science and Technology] at [Anhui University].

My current research interests include image matching, graph data representation and learning and few-shot learning. I have published more than 80 papers including 30+ papers in top conference and journal, such as IEEE T-PAMI, IJCV, IEEE T-MM, CVPR, NeurIPS and ACM-MM. I gained some research projects of NSFC and Natural Science Foundation of Anhui Province. I also received the rising star award of ACM Hefei.

Research Topic

  • Feature Matching: Graph matching, Image matching

  • Graph Representation Learning: Graph neural network, Graph embedding, Semi-supervised learning

  • Visual Representation: Few-shot learning, Graph based visual representation
